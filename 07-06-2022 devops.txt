----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. What is DevOps?

ANS- A compound of development (Dev) and operations (Ops), DevOps is the union of people, process and 
technology to continually provide value to customers.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Who are all the stakeholders in Development teams? 

ANS- Architect,Business representatives,Customers,Product Owners,Projects managers,Quality Assurance(QA),Testers and Analyst suppliers.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Who are all the stakeholders in Operation teams?

ANS- Information security professionals,Systems engineers,System administrators,IT operations engineers,release engineers,
database administrators (DBAS),networks engineers,support professionals,third party vendors and suppiler
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. What is Information technology?

ANS- Information technology(IT) is the use of computer systems or devices to access information.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. What is a software?

ANS-Software is used to control a computer.
   Examples-quicksbooks,adobe acrobat,google chrome,microsoft word etc.

6.Types of Softwares, define them and share few examples?

ANS- There are two types software. 1.System software 2.Application software
1.System software:-
programs that controls the  operations of the computer and its devices. eg-android,Mac operating systems,Ms windows etc. 
2.Application software:-
programs that performs specific tasks for users.eg-word processor,games,media players etc.
